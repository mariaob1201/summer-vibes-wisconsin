Quick Tips

  • 🕑 Best time: Late June–August; July has warmest lake temps (70°F+). Book 4–6 weeks ahead.
  • ⚓ Renting a boat: Bring a valid ID; pontoons run $80–$200/day at most marinas.
  • 👪 With kids: Pack sunscreen, life jackets, and check if beaches have lifeguards.
  • 🏝 Hiking: Wear layers, bring bug spray, and download AllTrails offline maps.
  • 🎣 Fishing license: Required for ages 16+; 1-day non-resident ~$10 at wildlifelicense.com/wi.
  • 🌟 Hidden gem: Visit Tue–Thu for quieter beaches and easier boat rentals.
